About EasyFood

Your trusted platform for food discovery

What is EasyFood?

EasyFood is a food discovery platform designed to help tourists and locals find where to eat easily when traveling between cities or states. We focus on providing comprehensive information about restaurants, their cuisines, menus, and locations to help you make informed decisions about where to dine.

Who is EasyFood for?

EasyFood serves three primary user groups:

  • Tourists visiting Kuala Lumpur who want to discover local dining experiences
  • Locals traveling between states seeking familiar or new culinary experiences
  • Food explorers looking for trusted places to eat in unfamiliar areas

Our Mission

We aim to simplify the process of finding quality dining establishments by providing accurate, up-to-date information about restaurants across Malaysia. Our platform allows users to search, browse, and discover restaurants based on location, cuisine type, price range, and dietary preferences.

Important Notice

EasyFood is a discovery platform, not a food delivery service. We do not handle food orders or deliveries. Our goal is to connect you with restaurants where you can enjoy a great dining experience in person.

Why Choose EasyFood?

  • Comprehensive restaurant information
  • Detailed menu listings
  • Halal and dietary indicators
  • User-friendly interface
  • Mobile-optimized experience
  • Accurate location and directions
